SmartGuide

User Manuals Made Simple

Products increase in complexity, so does the length of the user manuals! Finding the right piece of information requires more and more effort from an average customer, who may choose not to read manuals at all. Add SmartGuide to your website and enjoy the benefits!

How does it work?

Step 1: You need information from a manual

Great, just type the question into the search box! SmartGuide provides a simple user interface widget, which you can integrate into your site.

Step 2: SmartGuide analyzes your question

The algorithm, powered by state-of-art machine learning and NLP engine, processes the text to understand the meaning of the question.

Step 3: You get back the most relevant content

SmartGuide first "speed reads" the user manual. It looks for sentences, which can answer your question. Finally it returns all sections, which might give you the right answers.
